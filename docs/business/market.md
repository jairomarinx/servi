# Market - servi.cam

## Target
- Mid-sized Colombian cities: Ibague, Manizales, Pereira, Bucaramanga
- Global expansion after local validation

## Core insight
- High urgency local needs: plumber today, overnight nurse, appliance repair
- Large informal talent pool with no platform access
- High friction between supply and demand
- No dominant player in this niche in Colombia

## Competition
| Platform | Focus | Difference |
|----------|-------|------------|
| Freelancer.com | Remote digital | We do in-person local |
| Upwork | Remote digital | Same |
| Rappi | Delivery | We do skilled services |
| Facebook Marketplace | General | We have trust system |

## Differentiators
- Skill-match feed not just search
- Trust score with optional national ID
- Urgency system for immediate needs
- Built for informal skilled workers
